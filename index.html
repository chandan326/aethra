❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sell my digital creations? +
When uploading a new post (Sticker pack, Live Wallpaper, etc.), change the Pricing option to 'Paid' and set your desired price in Rupees (₹). Once published, it will automatically list in the Aethra Marketplace.
How does the AI assistant work? +
Aethra AI is powered by Google Gemini. You can open the chat panel using the floating chat button in the bottom right corner of the screen and ask any questions about content creation, hashtags, AI tools, or growing your audience.
Can I upload Live Wallpapers? +
Yes! Simply select the 'Live Wallpaper' content type in the creation modal, upload your animation/video or high-res layout file, and it will list in the Explore section under Live Wallpapers.
When will support reply to my ticket? +
Our dedicated support team reviews all submissions within 24 hours. You will receive an email confirmation and updates sent to your registered email address.
